Goal/s

The goal of this website is to provide the client, U3A Townsville with a new, updated and improved site in order to generate more traffic and attention from potential new members and benefactors. This goal will be measured by the number of new members that the organisation receives in the coming months, as well as any increase in the number of benefactors/donations that the organisation receives. General web traffic can be used as an indication of how many more people the site is attracting in comparison to the old site, in order to see if the changes made have been worthwhile.
